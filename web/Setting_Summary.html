Warranty Coverage

Yaniga Diamondoffers a complimentary 3-year limited warranty on all fine jewelry purchases. This warranty covers quality issues resulting from manufacturing defects, including:

  • Loose or misaligned stones caused by faulty settings
  • Defective prongs or mountings
  • Broken clasps, soldering issues, or structural flaws

What's Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear (e.g., scratches, dents, fading, tarnishing)
  • Damage caused by accidents, improper care, or unauthorized third-party repairs

At Yaniga Diamond, we are committed to delivering high-quality diamond jewelry and a smooth shopping experience. While we do not offer refunds, we do provide an exchange policy within 7 days of purchase for eligible items.

Exchange Policy

  • Exchanges must be requested within 7 days of the original purchase date.
  • Custom-made items are not eligible for exchange

Conditions for Exchange

To be eligible for an exchange, the item must meet the following conditions:

  • The original packaging must be included and in like-new condition.
  • All tags, stickers, and identifying labels must be intact and undamaged.
  • All accompanying documentation (certificates, invoices, etc.) must be included and in pristine condition.
  • The product itself must be undamaged and in like-new condition.
  • A valid receipt must be presented as proof of purchase.
